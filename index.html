About Us

Hi there ! We are Team Coding aka Team C.

Our idea for this website was to provide a solution to countless hours of browsing for a good movie or show to watch.

With so many streaming devices we spend a lot of time browsing threw content finding the right movie to watch. So our team came up with a solution. Based on your mood, we will suggest you movies that you can watch saving valuable screen time. We also as a bonus suggest a food to go along with the movie and your mood.

We hope that you enjoy using our website . If you have any feedback please feel free to contact us.
